Baked Potato

Description

This recipe is a staple in any chefs repertoire. Easy, quick, and delicious, this dish will be a fan favorite at any dinner party.

Preperation and cooking is simple. All you need is a potato, an oven, and some butter and green onions to top this tuber.

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Butter
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 350
  2. Put the potato in for 15 minutes
  3. Remove the potato
  4. Cut it open and add butter, salt and pepper, and green onions
