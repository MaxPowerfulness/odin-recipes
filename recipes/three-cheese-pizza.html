Three Cheese Pizza

Description

This recipe dates back centries ago to a little town along the Adriatic, passed down from generation to generation, from father to son, mother to daughter, father to daughter, mother to son.

The recipe calls for three of the finest cheeses, mixed together into a cheesy flurry, blending the delicious flavors into one. Personally, I like to use blue cheese, nacho cheese, and cream cheese. Throw them together in a bowl, mix it, and slap it on some dough and call it good.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Marinara sauce
  • Blue Cheese
  • Nacho Cheese
  • Cream Cheese
  • Oven

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 374
  2. Spread the dough into a thing circle
  3. Lather some marinara sauce until it completely covers the dough
  4. Mix your cheese in a mixing bowl until it is a homogeneous abomination
  5. Put the cheese on the dough
  6. Toss the pizza in the oven and wait 18 minutes
  7. Bone apple tea
